How It Works

Three steps to a safer walk.

① Enter destination

Drop a pin or search your destination — StepShield finds your current location automatically.

② AI analyzes the route

Weather, time of day, lighting, road type, crosswalks and community reports are scored in real time.

③ Receive the safest route

Get a clear Safety Score, risk level, plain-language explanation, and a safer alternative if one exists.

The Innovation

Fastest isn't always safest.

Every other navigation app optimizes for time. StepShield is the first to optimize for you — scoring each route against the conditions that actually cause pedestrian injuries.

Live condition scoring

Community-verified reports
